French Toast

Ingredients

8 inches of french bread (sliced into eight, one inch slices) 4 eggs 1 cup of milk 1 Tbsp sugar 2 Tbsp Butter

Directions

Mix the egg, sugar and milk in a large shallow bowl. beat with a fork until theres no more visual egg chunks. butter a frying pan on medium high heat. put a slice of french bread in the egg milk for a minute on both sides then into the pan for 3 minutes on each side, do this for each piece. serve with syrup and powdered sugar

notes

try to time the bread soaking with the pan flipping like
  • bread into the mix
  • flip bread in mix
  • bread into the pan
  • flip the bread already in the pan
    • you can usually fit a few breads in the egg container and on the pan, fit as much as possible.
