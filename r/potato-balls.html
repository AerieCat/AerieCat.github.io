Potato Balls

These balls are good snacks and are very filling

ingredients

  • 3 large potatoes
  • seasonings of your choosing
  • a block of mozzerella cheese
  • breadcrumbs
  • 3 eggs
  • 4 Tbsp flour

    • instructions

    boil your peeled potatoes until they are mashable then mash them in a bowl with your seasonings of choice. Next make them into little balls and put a small piece of cheese cut from the block inside. Now cover them in egg and breadcrumbs and put int hte airfrier for about 10-15 minutes.

    notes:

    to reheat, put in the airfrier on a lower heat (200F) for 10 minutes, you want to melt the cheese inside
