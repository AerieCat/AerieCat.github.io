Rice Pudding

Ingredients

1 cup long-grain white rice 4 cups whole milk 1/2 cup sugar 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

Rinse the rice under cold water then pour the rice and milk into a saucepan, bring it to a simmer on medium heat then lower it back down to low heat until the rice is cooked. now add the sugar, vanilla, and cinamon and stir it, cook it a bit longer until it's at the thickness you want.

notes

  • Keep stiring atleast once every 2 minutes
  • you might want to add more milk if you want a thin pudding, the 4 cups was really thick and it was more like sticky rice
