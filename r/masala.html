Masala Tea

] This is gonna be a quick one, but i make this alot because it's so good and simple

Ingredients

  • 10 cardamon pods
  • 10 whole cloves
  • about an inch of ginger root
  • 2 black tea bags
  • Sugar to taste
  • 2 cup water
  • 1 cup milk

    • cooking

    Bring the water to a boil and put it in a teapot (or other container). crush the cadamon and cloves enough to where they are open, then throw them in a coffee filter with the ginger, cut into small slices. close the coffee filter up with a twist tie and throw it in the water with the black tea bags. Let it steap for about 5 minutes. heat up the milk in the microwave for a minute and a half then throw it in the pot with the tea after it's steaped. Stir it about and add sugar to taste (i do about 1/3 a cup)) let it sit for 5 more minutes then poor a little for a taste test. If it is strong enough, remove the tea bag and spices, otherwise keep them in for longer.
