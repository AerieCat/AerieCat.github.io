Non-Queer KSU community resources

University, mutual aid, and community based resources

  • KSU buy nothing group (Group Me)

    • a Buy Nothing Group is a "a hyper-local, virtual community where neighbors can give and receive items, services, and skills for free, fostering a sharing and gifting economy, and aiming to reduce consumption and build community"

  • KSU CARE services

    • KSU Cares is a university run food pantry you can visit to get free food from bi-weekly. They also provide "access to food, temporary housing, and other supportive services to foster students' realization of a healthy, stable, and dignified life." on a case by case manner.

  • Owl Swap

    • Owl Swap has 2 free clothes closets on either campus where students can go to get free, gently used clothes
