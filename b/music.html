Diary of Dolls and Death

By: Bambi Baker

Genre: Morute

Description: This album was originally recommended to me by a friend. It has very dark themes of abuse and mental issues. It peaks my interest in psychology i've had to gain since my mental problems became uncovered. The song Derealization is especially prevalent to me because it feels like she writes it out of experience. The music itself is not very intense, it's slow and leans heavily on piano and similar instruments. The lyrics hit like a train though. They aren't hidden behind metaphors or anything like most heavier songs are. It is mostly in simple plain english.
