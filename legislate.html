introduction

In the past few weeks, numerous anti-trans laws have poassed in the southern states. In tennesee, HB0001, the first bill of the 2023 session, was a bill passed to strip the rights of minors to gender affirming care. This bill was filed for introduction November 9th, 2022 and signed by the govenor March 2nd, 2023 to become law March 22nd, 2023. In Georgia, GA SB140 was passed doing the exact same thing. The Georgia website doesn't give a date for filed for introduction, but it does say it was read to the senate Febuary 13th, 2023 and signed by the govenor on March 23rd, 2023 to become law imediately, only 1 day after the Tennesee one became law. The Georgia bill only took 38 days from firstread to inshrined as law.

Georgia's bill restricts:
"(1) Sex reassignment surgeries, or any other surgical procedures, that are performed for the purpose of altering primary or secondary sexual characteristics" and
"(2) Hormone replacement therapies."
Notice the order. The Georgia bill lists surgery first. I could not find any statistics on how many children in these states were given Gender affirming Surgery, but most Hospitals require you to be 1 year on HRT before you can get on the waiting list for the surgery. You could imagine that not many children have been treated.
